# Fikxi - Product Vision

## Problem

In cities like Mumbai, customers often struggle to find trusted local workers for everyday services such as AC repair, courier delivery, car repair, plumbing, electrician work, and home repairs.

At the same time, many skilled workers struggle to find consistent work, fair pricing, and direct access to customers or companies.

Many workers may also not be comfortable with English-heavy or complicated apps.

## Solution

Fikxi is a multilingual local services marketplace that allows customers to post service jobs and allows verified workers to find, bid, negotiate, schedule, and complete those jobs.

The platform supports English, Hindi, and Marathi so that workers and customers can use the system comfortably.

## Mission

To make local services more trusted, accessible, and fair for both customers and workers.

## Target City

Initial launch focus: Mumbai, India.

## Target Users

### Customers

People who want local services completed from home, such as:

- AC repair
- Home repair
- Car repair
- Courier delivery
- Electrician work
- Plumbing work

### Workers

Skilled or semi-skilled workers who want more job opportunities, such as:

- AC technicians
- Electricians
- Plumbers
- Mechanics
- Delivery workers
- Home repair workers

### Companies

Businesses that need verified workers for larger jobs, bulk hiring, maintenance work, delivery support, or temporary labour.

## Key Differentiators

- Multilingual from the beginning
- Worker-friendly interface
- Job bidding and negotiation
- Verified worker profiles
- Secure payment flow
- Support for individual customers and companies
- Designed for Mumbai's local service economy

## Long-Term Vision

Fikxi can grow into a trusted platform where customers, workers, and companies can connect for any local service work across India.
